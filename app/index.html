About

Norma

"Creativity is intelligence having fun !" – Albert Einstein
And I’ve been living it since ever… Engaged in many social and extra-curricular activities, I’ve always had an urge to innovate and introduce new ways to organize common activities. Constantly mixing creativity and perfectionism, I believe that originality lies in the smallest details… Driven by this thrive to break the trends and take this energy to a higher level, I have joined forces with my best friend, Nathalie, to create Tangram… our way of shaping events.
